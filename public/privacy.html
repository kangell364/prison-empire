Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026 — TEMPLATE: review & edit before publishing to the stores.

This Privacy Policy explains how Prison Empire (“we”, “the app”) collects and uses information when you play. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account email — only if you choose to create an account. Used to sign you in and recover your account.
  • Game progress — your in-game profile, resources, and progression, stored so you can play across devices.
  • Anonymous identifier — a random ID assigned to guest players to save progress before an account is created.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not collect your real name, contacts, photos, or precise location unless a feature you use explicitly asks for it.

How we use it

To run the game, save and sync your progress, authenticate your account, and prevent abuse.

Where it’s stored

Account and progress data is stored with our backend provider, Supabase. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections.

Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from inside the app: Profile → Account → Delete Account. This action is immediate and irreversible. You may also email us at the address below to request deletion.

Children’s privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their data.
