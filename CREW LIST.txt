WHAT THIS IS
  The 100 COMMON crew cards — the floor of the collection. Every card is one
  individual street-gang member with art, an ATK number, a DEF number, and a
  flavor Bonus + Weakness. These stack (20 -> merge to next card level, max
  Lv60) and get per-level ATK/DEF upgrades (LV/20) exactly like the existing
  player cards. They fight other PLAYERS' crews, so balance matters.

  Cards are numbered CARD 1 .. CARD 100. A "(suggested: ...)" street name is
  kept on each for when you art/name them — rename freely. Bonus/Weakness are
  FLAVOR for now (no combat effect wired yet), written in a consistent
  condition-based format so they wire into the battle engine later.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BALANCE FRAMEWORK  (why the numbers look the way they do)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Commons are deliberately weak so Rare/Epic/Legendary have room to climb. Power
  total = ATK + DEF. Ability strength — NOT raw stats — is what should separate
  a Common from a Legendary (this is how Hearthstone/Clash/Magic do it).

      TIER         POWER TOTAL   MAX SINGLE STAT   ABILITY STRENGTH
      ---------    -----------   ---------------   ------------------------------
   >> COMMON          50 – 70          52          tiny bonus, real weakness (net ~0)
      UNCOMMON        70 – 95          70          one modest bonus, soft weakness
      RARE            95 – 130         85          strong bonus, minor weakness
      EPIC           130 – 175        110          big bonus, cosmetic weakness
      LEGENDARY      175 – 230+       130          powerful bonus, little/no weakness

  KEEP THIS IN MIND: if a Common creeps above ~70 total or gets a strong,
  un-paired bonus, it eats into the Uncommon/Rare design space and makes the
  chase cards pointless in PvP. Every Common bonus below is small and is paid
  for with a matching weakness.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ARCHETYPE LEGEND  (the shape of each card's stat split)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  [RUNNER]  glass cannon — very high ATK, very low DEF   (hits hard, folds fast)
  [BRUISER] aggressive   — high ATK, low DEF
  [BRAWLER] balanced     — even ATK / DEF
  [TILT-A]  ATK-leaning balanced
  [TILT-D]  DEF-leaning balanced
  [WALL]    defensive    — low ATK, high DEF
  [BODY]    heavy tank   — very low ATK, very high DEF  (soaks hits, barely scratches)

  LOOKS  (new): a short, comma-separated physical descriptor for each card —
  height, build, complexion, age, and one stand-out feature — in the same
  short-keyword style as CARD BUILDS.txt. The Art line folds those traits in so
  one line is a complete prompt. Keep the original art scene; just make sure the
  person's build/height/complexion is stated.

  Format:
    CARD ###   (suggested: name)        [ARCHETYPE]   ATK ##  DEF ##   (total)
         Bonus:    + condition-based upside
         Weakness: - condition-based downside
         Looks:    height, build, complexion, age, stand-out feature
         Art:      one-line art brief (incorporates the Looks)
